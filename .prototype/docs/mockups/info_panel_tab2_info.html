DESCRIPTION

The fourth planet from Sol and humanity's first successful colonization target. Mars hosts a growing network of settlements, mining operations, and research stations. Its thin CO₂ atmosphere requires pressurized habitats, but abundant mineral resources and relatively mild gravity have made it the industrial backbone of the inner system.
